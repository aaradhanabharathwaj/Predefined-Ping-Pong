Ping Pong



















Instructions:

1. First, keep your Laptop in an Upright Position.

2. Move yourself away form the System about 3 - 4 Feet back.

3. Move your Right Wrist in an Ascending and Descending Manner, a Red Dot is to appear on your Right Wrist.

4. Now, click on the Red Button and move back.

5. The Red Paddel is your Paddel and the Orange Paddel is the Computer's Paddel. Your Paddel with move according to the Movement of your Right Wrist.

ENJOY!
